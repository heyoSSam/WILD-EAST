About

During the second half of SALUKI's "Like Home" tour, Arseniy's fans began to notice a strange masked man at concerts and on social networks who did not reveal his identity. There were a lot of guesses about this, but the main thing was the beginning of the promotional campaign for the new SALUKI album. Soon, on October 15, 2021, the first phase was released, a promo for the album “WILD EA$T” entitled: “DR. ANGELDUST / PHASE I”[21], in which this “masked man” appeared. The first act of the promo was released every Monday for several months. The second act of promos was planned to begin in 2022, but was never released.
